Cookie Policy

This page describes cookies and similar technologies used on saurabh374.github.io. Optional analytical and marketing cookies are not loaded without your consent.

Effective from: June 18, 2026

What are cookies

Cookies are small files stored in your browser. Browser local storage serves a similar purpose. On this site, they are used to remember user preferences, measure website traffic, and load promotional features according to your preferences.

Categories

Necessary Saves cookie consent choices, theme, and language. Without these, preferences cannot be remembered. Always active
Analytical Google Analytics tracks basic visitor metrics and performance. Consent required
Marketing Google AdSense or advertising tags for personalized ad tools. Consent required

Specific Technologies

portfolio_cookie_consent Stores choice settings for necessary, analytical, and marketing consents. 180 days
portfolio.lang Remembers chosen language preference in browser local storage. Persistent
portfolio.theme Remembers chosen UI theme (dark/light) in browser local storage. Persistent
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics identifiers used to track traffic. Google settings
__gads, __gpi, IDE etc. Google AdSense or marketing ad tags loaded after marketing consent. Google settings

Managing Your Choices

You can change your consent settings at any time by clicking the "Cookie settings" button in the footer of the main page. You can reject optional cookies without losing access to the website. Cookies and local storage data can also be deleted directly in your browser settings.

Contact Form and External Services

Submitting the contact form sends data via the FormSubmit service. External links (GitHub, LinkedIn) may set their own cookies once you click to visit those platforms.
